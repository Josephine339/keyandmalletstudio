Lessons

LESSON TYPES
  • Beginner
    • Starting a new instrument and don't know where to start in percussion?
  • School support
    • Attending the school's band and your conductor has requested you to complete certain tunes or pieces? I will guide students to complete these requirements.
  • Exam prep
    • Starting exams and need a teacher to be able to enroll?
LESSON LENGTH (30 / 45 MIN)
  • I offer lessons of 30 minutes or 45 minutes in duration
  • First few lessons will be 30 minutes in duration for me to understand the student and the student to understand my teaching style.
  • Trial lesson is available for 30 minutes with a parent-tutor interaction available before and after the lesson.
